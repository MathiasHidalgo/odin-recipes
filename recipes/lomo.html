Lomo Saltado

Description:

Lomo saltado is a traditional Peruvian food, a beef steak with white rice and fries, mixed with tomato and red onions

Ingredients:

    beef white rice potato for fries tomatoes red onions

Steps

  • Cook the beef according to package instructions
  • Warm the white rice in a large pot
  • Cook the potatoes until they are tender
  • Add the cooked beef, tomatoes, red onions, and cooked potatoes to the rice
  • Cook for 20-25 minutes until the beef is tender and the rice is cooked through
  • Serve with fries
